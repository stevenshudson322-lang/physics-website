Roller Coaster Physics

Energy conservation determines whether the coaster completes the course. At any point: KE = E_total − PE − E_thermal. The cart speeds up going downhill (PE→KE) and slows going uphill (KE→PE).

Friction continuously removes mechanical energy. Each metre of track costs energy equal to μmg·cos(θ)·ds. With enough friction, the cart stalls before reaching high points.

Build your own track by clicking 🔧 Build Track and dragging the yellow control points. The simulation automatically fits a smooth curve through your points.
